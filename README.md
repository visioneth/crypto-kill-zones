## What Are Kill Zones

Kill zones are recurring time windows where institutional order flow creates predictable price pressure. These align with global market open and close cycles. The same institutions execute at the same times every session and price moves with them.

This is not a mystical pattern. It is institutional schedule arbitrage.

## Why 20:00 UTC Specifically

20:00 UTC is when the US afternoon session overlaps with Asian pre-market positioning. Institutional desks in New York are closing positions. Asian desks are opening. The net flow during this window is overwhelmingly bearish across Bitcoin, Ethereum, and major altcoins.

The pattern holds across all market conditions: bull market, bear market, and sideways chop.

## How to Use This

Open a SHORT position 5-10 minutes before the kill zone window opens. Use a tight stop loss based on ATR rather than a fixed percentage. Close when you have profit or let the SL work.

Do not trade against the bias during a kill zone. The edge disappears when you fight the direction.
